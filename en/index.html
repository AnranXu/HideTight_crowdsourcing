Task

Your task is to upload (by clicking button ) 10 photos that you once shared or plan to share on social network service platforms (e.g. FaceBook, Twitter, Instagram, etc..) and annotate potential privacy-threatening content in your photos as much as you can.

Each photo should contain at least one privacy-threatening content.

Click the button "Brush" and hold your mouse to areas you want to add mosaic.

Please add one annotation box after you finish to hide one content.

If you want to remove the mosaic you have added, click the button "Eraser" and hold your mouse to areas you want to remove mosaic.

You may also adjust the size of brushstroke and mosaic through the slides as you like.

Tips:

  • You may upload photos that do not contain any person.

  • Do not upload photos that only contain scenery.

  • Do not upload several photos taken in the same place.

  • If possible, try to upload photos with different themes.

  • You may go back to read the instruction at any time so do not worry about being a little bit confused at this stage.
